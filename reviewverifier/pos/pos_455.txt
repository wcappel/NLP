to dedicated power source 110 volt outlet.It is not recommended that

accordance to sound electrical application. Do not overload or connect grounding 
cheaters in non-grounded 110 volt systems.Use with stand alone generators
 should be closely monitored and PowerSquid not be used beyond generator's
